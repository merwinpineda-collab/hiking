Trail Overview
Description

The TrailMate hiking trail offers 8 kilometers of scenic adventure across lush forests, rivers, and panoramic viewpoints. It's designed for beginners and intermediate hikers looking for a refreshing outdoor escape.

Location & Direction

Located in Sierra Madre, Rizal. From Manila, take the Marilaque Highway until you reach the Tanay junction, then follow the signs leading to TrailMate Trailhead. Parking and shuttle services are available.

Rules & Regulations
Always stay on marked trails.
Respect wildlife and avoid disturbing natural habitats.
No littering — bring your trash back.
Open fires are prohibited outside designated areas.
Fees Per Person
₱150 / person
Covers maintenance, ranger assistance, and insurance.
Operation Hours
6:00 AM – 6:00 PM
Monday – Sunday, all year round.
Safety Reminders
  • Wear proper footwear and bring water.
  • Inform a companion before hiking.
  • Follow your guide's instructions.
  • Check the weather forecast before the trip.
